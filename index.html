Strength & Build Program

Weekly Plan

The aim: get stronger and fill out. Light-to-moderate weights, clean form, 8–15 reps. Slowly add a rep or a little weight each week. Eat enough and sleep well — that's where the growth happens. Tap any exercise to see how to do it.

The Week
Five rules
1
Form before weightFirst two weeks, go light just to learn the movements. Always.
2
Progress slowlyHit the top of the rep range with good form twice? Add a rep or a little weight.
3
Food is half the jobEat more than usual — protein every meal: eggs, chicken, mince, milk, yogurt, tuna, peanut butter.
4
Sleep 9–10 hoursGrowth and muscle repair happen while sleeping. Non-negotiable.
5
Listen to the bodyMuscle soreness is fine. Sharp joint pain is not — stop that exercise. No max heavy lifts while still growing.
